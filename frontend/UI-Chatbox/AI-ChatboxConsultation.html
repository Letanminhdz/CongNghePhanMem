Amoxicillin is a common penicillin antibiotic. Here is the information regarding your questions:

Common Side Effects:

  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Mild skin rash
  • Upset stomach

Taking with Food:

Yes, it is generally recommended to take Amoxicillin with food or a meal. Taking it with food can help prevent stomach upset and nausea, which are common side effects.

Important: If you develop a severe skin rash, itching, difficulty breathing, or swelling of the face/throat, seek emergency medical attention immediately as these are signs of an allergic reaction.
